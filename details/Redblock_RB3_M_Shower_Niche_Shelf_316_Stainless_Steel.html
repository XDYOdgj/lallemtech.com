Redblock RB3-M Shower Niche Shelf

Enhance your bathroom with the Redblock RB3-M, a durable and stylish niche shelf built for wet environments. Designed specifically for 12-inch prefab niches, this shelf is made from Marine Grade 316 stainless steel, offering superior corrosion resistance and long-term performance even in the harshest conditions.

Key Features

  • Constructed from corrosion-resistant 316 stainless steel for longevity
  • 3mm thickness ensures strength and a secure fit in shower spaces
  • Built-in washcloth and loofah hangers for easy access and organization
  • Designed for 12-inch niches with 1/8-inch grout lines
  • Meets industry standards for stain and soap scum resistance

Why Choose RB3-M?

Whether you're building a new bathroom or remodeling an existing one, the RB3-M offers a clean, modern solution that fits seamlessly with tile and waterproofing systems. With a 1/2-inch gap behind the shelf, it keeps your space hygienic by preventing soap scum accumulation. This product is ideal for installation during tile work, offering a hassle-free and elegant storage option without requiring retrofitting in finished showers.
