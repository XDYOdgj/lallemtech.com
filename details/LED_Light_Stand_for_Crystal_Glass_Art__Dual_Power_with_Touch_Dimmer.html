LED Light Stand for Crystal and Glass Art

Elevate your crystal and glass art displays with a versatile and elegant lighting solution. This light stand is engineered with a dual power system, letting you choose between 3AA batteries for on-the-go use or a USB connection for a stable, in-place setup. Whether you're arranging intricate glass pieces or highlighting a stunning crystal sculpture, the brightness and color tones can be easily adjusted with the touch dimmer. With options for cool white (5000K) and warm amber (3000K) LEDs, you can create the perfect lighting atmosphere for any display.

Key Features

  • Dual power system for flexibility: operate with 3AA batteries or USB cable
  • Touch dimmer for adjustable brightness control (10%-100%)
  • Sturdy wood base with rounded edges and non-slip surface
  • Compact 4.7\" size for easy placement on shelves or tables
  • Cord-free design for safe and clutter-free crystal display arrangements

Why Choose This Stand?

Designed for both professionals and collectors, this LED light stand provides optimal illumination for crystal and glass art. Its solid wood construction ensures durability and stability, while the modern, rounded design adds a touch of sophistication. The inclusion of a USB cable enhances convenience, making it a great fit for long-term use in your home or workspace. Whether you're curating a gallery or simply showcasing beautiful art in your living room, this stand delivers both form and function.

The adjustable brightness feature allows you to tailor the lighting to your specific needs, from subtle ambient glow to bright, focused illumination. This is perfect for creating the right mood or enhancing the visual appeal of your crystal and glass masterpieces. A lightweight yet strong design ensures it's easy to move but holds up to 11lbs (5kg) of display items securely.
