Elevate Your Lighting with Uxcell Candle-Shaped Socket Covers

Add a touch of sophistication and warmth to your chandeliers and decorative lamps with these beautifully crafted Uxcell socket covers. Designed to look like real candles with detailed wax drops, they bring elegance and charm to any lighting setup.

Engineered from durable iron and finished with a high-temperature electroplating process, these covers are built to last while offering excellent rust resistance. They are the perfect fit for E14 candelabra base bulbs, ensuring a secure and stylish connection.

Standing at 3 inches tall with a 25mm outer diameter, each cover is designed for a snug fit on standard lamp holders. Whether you're creating a new lighting design or upgrading an existing one, these covers are ideal for both residential and commercial spaces.

Each pack contains 10 covers, providing ample options for multiple installations. Always remember to insulate the lamp holder and sleeve during setup to guarantee safety and optimal performance.
