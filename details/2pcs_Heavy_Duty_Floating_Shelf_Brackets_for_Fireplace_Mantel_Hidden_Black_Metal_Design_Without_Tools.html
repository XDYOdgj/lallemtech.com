Craftersmark 2pcs Floating Shelf Brackets

These robust floating shelf brackets are built with precision and durability, making them the perfect choice for adding sleek storage solutions to your home. Designed for 1-inch thick backplates, they are compatible with a variety of wood types such as lumber, butcher block, and thin wood, offering versatility for any project. Crafted from heavy-duty 5mm galvanized steel, these brackets deliver exceptional strength and reliability, ensuring your shelves are secure and stable for years to come.

Each bracket features pre-drilled and adjustable holes, which make aligning with the studs simple and efficient, reducing installation time. The fully concealed design keeps brackets hidden within the wood, providing a clean and elegant look that enhances your interior style without sacrificing functionality. Ideal for both residential and commercial use, these brackets are the perfect addition to any space where organization and aesthetics matter.
