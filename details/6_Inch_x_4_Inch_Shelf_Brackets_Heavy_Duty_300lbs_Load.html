Sturdy 6" x 4" Shelf Brackets for Heavy-Duty Use

These 6 inch x 4 inch shelf brackets are crafted from heavy-duty alloy metal, combining strength with lightweight design to make them perfect for both residential and commercial setups. They offer a secure 90-degree vertical angle, ensuring your shelves remain stable and safe even under heavy loads. With a maximum holding capacity of 300 pounds per pair, they're ideal for creating floating shelves that can support household essentials, tools, or displays without compromising the integrity of your walls.

Whether you're organizing your farmhouse kitchen, reinforcing your garage storage, or upgrading your basement setup, these brackets provide an elegant and functional way to transform your space. Their powder-coated matte black finish is not only durable against scratches and wear but also complements a variety of interior design styles, from rustic to modern. Plus, the included G5 high-strength screws make installation effortless, especially for hard walls, ensuring a neat and permanent fit.
