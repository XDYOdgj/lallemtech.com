CarShelf Heavy Duty Wall Storage Racks

Maximize your garage space with these sleek and robust wall storage racks. Designed to hold heavy items like bins, boxes, tires, and tools with ease, they combine durability with a modern aesthetic to make organizing your workshop or home garage simple and effective.

Why Choose CarShelf?

Engineered for strength and long-term use, these metal floating shelves are built to last. Their reinforced triangular frame and heavy-gauge steel construction ensure stability even under significant weight, while the rust-proof galvanized steel panels make them perfect for outdoor storage solutions. Whether you're working on a car, DIY projects, or just need extra space, these racks offer the reliability you need.

Key Features

  • Supports over 1000 lbs total weight with 5 sturdy braces
  • Easy solo installation with included drilling template and bubble level
  • Removable edge barriers to prevent small items from slipping
  • Ultra-sturdy design with reinforced triangular frame and heavy-gauge steel
  • Rust-proof galvanized steel panels for long-lasting outdoor use

Versatile Installation Options

These wall mounted storage racks can be installed vertically, in corners, or side-by-side, offering flexible storage solutions for any garage or workshop layout. Their space-saving design makes them ideal for both indoor and outdoor use, helping you keep your essentials within reach while keeping your space organized.
