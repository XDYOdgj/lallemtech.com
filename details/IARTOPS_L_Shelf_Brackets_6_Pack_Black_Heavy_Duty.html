Enhance Your Space with Durable Floating Shelf Brackets

IARTOPS offers a reliable 6-pack of black L-shaped floating shelf brackets, crafted from heavy-duty iron with a long-lasting matte black powder coat finish. These brackets are built to withstand wear and tear, making them an excellent choice for maximizing storage in your home. Each bracket provides sturdy support for up to 80 pounds, ensuring your items stay secure and stable.

With their 90-degree angle design, these brackets help maintain level placement and prevent tipping, creating a safe and organized storage system. Whether you're arranging books, plants, or small storage baskets, these brackets offer a versatile and modern solution that complements various interior styles. They are ideal for DIY projects and can be installed on walls made of drywall, brick, or concrete for added flexibility.

Our floating shelf brackets are designed with ease of installation in mind. All necessary hardware, including screws and wall anchors, is included in the package, making it simple to set up and complete your project quickly. These brackets are perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and even corner spaces, allowing you to create a customized storage arrangement tailored to your needs.
